Ape Escape: Survival
A fast-paced, retro-inspired arcade game where timing is everything and size definitely matters. Control a hungry chimp, eat bananas, and try to survive as long as possible!

🕹️ Gameplay:
Eat Bananas: Every banana you grab grows your size and increases your score.

Dodge Obstacles: As you get bigger, dodging boulders on the ground becomes a massive challenge!

Survive: High risk, high reward—watch the "Survival Time" clock and chase a new high score.

🛠️ How to Play:
Open https://mnsvi.github.io/Ape-Escape-Survival/ in your browser.

Move the monkey to catch floating bananas.

Dodge incoming obstacles.
